About Us

Sweet Affairs is a Dessert parlour that’s been serving familiar delights with a unique twist, shaking up the dessert scene in Southall. We make our desserts the way they’re meant to, using premium ingredients and carefully curated recipes that work together to invoke unfiltered joy. The kids’ play area is a treat amongst treats for those who visit us with their little bundles of happiness. We distinguish ourselves beyond desserts by offering great customer service whether you dine-in, take-out, or have your meal delivered.

From Ice Cream and Gelato, to Cakes, Waffles, Crepes and Shakes, you're just a click away from enjoying everything from our awesome menu available for convenient pick-up. Our goal is to make your online experience Sweeter and more pleasurable, and we look forward to serving you better.

We at Sweet Affairs are devoted to creating a dessert experience that puts your satisfaction above all else. Unlock the Sweetness now!
